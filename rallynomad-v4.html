RALLYNOMAD V4

Introducing the RallyNomad V4, a motorcycle engineered for adventure enthusiasts who demand cutting-edge technology and superior performance. Designed to excel in the most challenging terrains and conditions, the RallyNomad V4 combines precision engineering with innovative safety and comfort features. From advanced suspension systems to intelligent safety controls, every element is crafted to ensure an unparalleled riding experience. Here’s a detailed look at the features that make the RallyNomad V4 the ultimate choice for riders who dare to push the limits.

Features

Engine Brake Control (EBC)

This system enhances stability during extreme cornering by balancing the forces acting on the rear tire when the engine brake is heavily applied, ensuring smooth and controlled handling.

ABS Cornering

The anti-lock braking system prevents wheel lock-up during intense braking, maintaining traction by regulating braking pressure. Its intervention adjusts based on the selected Riding Mode, offering optimal safety for various conditions.

Fenor Skyhook Suspension (FSS)

A semi-active suspension system that adapts to road conditions in real-time. It adjusts suspension response to maximize comfort, improve control, and minimize load transfer during hard braking or acceleration.

Daytime Running Light (DRL) and Fenor Cornering Light (FCL)

Advanced LED lighting that activates based on the bike's lean angle, illuminating the curve ahead for improved visibility. The DRL ensures compliance with daytime headlight requirements.

Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) and Forward Collision Warning

ACC automatically adjusts the bike’s speed to maintain a safe distance (selectable in four levels) from the vehicle in front, allowing effortless highway riding without throttle input. The Forward Collision Warning system alerts the rider to sudden proximity changes, enhancing safety.

Fenor Wheelie Control (DWC)

A feature designed to manage wheelies, enabling maximum acceleration with increased ease and safety for a more controlled and thrilling riding experience.
