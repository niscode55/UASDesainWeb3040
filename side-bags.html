Fennor Side Bag

Rp. 850.000

Material: Heavy-Duty Waterproof Canvas
Capacity: 20L per bag (40L total)
Mounting System: Adjustable Straps with Quick-Release Buckles
Features: Reinforced Seams, Reflective Elements, Multiple Compartments.

The **Fennor Side Bag** is your ultimate storage solution for long-distance rides, combining durability, style, and functionality. Made from **heavy-duty waterproof canvas**, this side bag is built to withstand challenging weather conditions while keeping your belongings safe and secure.

**Key Features:**
- **Large Capacity:** With 20L per bag (40L total), there's ample space for your travel essentials.
- **Reinforced Seams:** Ensures long-lasting durability and strength.
- **Quick-Release Buckles:** Easy to mount and detach, saving time and effort.
- **Multiple Compartments:** Organize your items efficiently with separate sections for different belongings.
- **Reflective Elements:** Increase visibility and safety during nighttime rides.

Designed for both functionality and style, the **Fennor Side Bag** is perfect for riders who demand high-quality gear that enhances their travel experience. Whether you're commuting or embarking on an adventurous journey, this side bag is an indispensable companion.
