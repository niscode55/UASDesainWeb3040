Fennor Tank Bag

Rp. 450.000

Material: Water-Resistant Polyester
Capacity: 10L - 15L Expandable
Mounting System: Magnetic and Strap
Features: Transparent Phone Pocket, Reflective Strips, Detachable Shoulder Strap.

The **Fennor Tank Bag** is a versatile and durable storage solution designed for riders who value convenience and functionality. Constructed from **water-resistant polyester**, this tank bag is perfect for storing essentials like your phone, wallet, and maps during your journey.

**Key Features:**
- **Expandable Capacity:** Adjust from 10L to 15L to fit more items as needed.
- **Transparent Phone Pocket:** Allows easy access to your phone or GPS while riding.
- **Magnetic and Strap Mounting:** Ensures a secure and stable fit on various tank sizes.
- **Reflective Strips:** Enhance visibility and safety during night rides.
- **Detachable Shoulder Strap:** Easily transform your tank bag into a shoulder bag for off-bike convenience.

Whether you're heading out on a long-distance trip or a quick ride around town, the **Fennor Tank Bag** is your reliable companion, combining style, practicality, and safety in one package.
