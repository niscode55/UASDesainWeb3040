Fennolube Sport

Rp. 50.000

Type: Fully Synthetic Motor Oil
Volume: 1 Liter
Viscosity Grade: 10W-40
Recommended Use: High-performance motorcycles

Experience unparalleled performance with **Fennolube Sport**, a fully synthetic motor oil designed specifically for high-performance motorcycles. Engineered with advanced lubrication technology, Fennolube Sport ensures optimal engine efficiency, durability, and superior thermal stability under extreme conditions.

**Key Benefits:**
- **Enhanced Engine Protection:** Reduces wear and tear, extending engine life.
- **Optimized Viscosity:** Maintains consistency for superior lubrication even at high temperatures.
- **Improved Acceleration:** Delivers smooth power transfer and performance.
- **Cleaner Engine:** Prevents sludge build-up, keeping your engine running at peak condition.

Ideal for riders who demand the best from their machines, Fennolube Sport provides outstanding protection and performance for both daily commutes and intense track rides. Invest in Fennolube Sport today and elevate your riding experience.
