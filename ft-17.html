FT-17

Meet the FT-17, a motorcycle crafted for riders who demand power, precision, and cutting-edge innovation. With a dynamic engine and advanced features, the FT-17 offers a seamless blend of performance and practicality, perfect for both urban commutes and thrilling adventures. From its powerful engine to its rider-centric technologies, every aspect of the FT-17 is designed to deliver an unparalleled riding experience. Here's an in-depth look at the features that set the FT-17 apart.

Features

155cc LC4V Engine with VVA

Delivers a maximum power of 14.2 kW at 10,000 rpm and torque of 14.7 Nm at 8,500 rpm. This 155cc engine features a 6-speed manual transmission, 4 valves, and Variable Valve Actuation (VVA) technology, ensuring consistent torque across all engine speeds. Liquid cooling maintains optimal engine temperature for superior performance.

Wide Handlebar with Big Bike Switch

A wide handlebar offers a more upright and comfortable riding position, enhancing overall riding control and experience.

Assist & Slipper Clutch

The assist feature reduces clutch effort, while the slipper clutch ensures smoother gear transitions and faster acceleration.

Projector LED Headlight

Equipped with projector LED technology, the headlight provides brighter and more focused illumination for better visibility.

Full Digital Speedometer with Shift Timing Light

A fully digital instrument panel paired with a 6-speed manual transmission and a shift timing light, enabling riders to achieve peak performance with ease.
