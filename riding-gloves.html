Fennor Riding Gloves

Rp. 250.000

Material: Genuine Leather with Mesh Inserts
Protection: Reinforced Knuckle Armor, Anti-Slip Palm Grip
Weather Resistance: Water-Resistant Coating
Sizes: S, M, L, XL
Features: Touchscreen Compatibility, Adjustable Wrist Strap.

The **Fennor Riding Gloves** are designed to provide ultimate comfort and protection for riders, combining high-quality materials and innovative features. These gloves are crafted from **genuine leather** with breathable mesh inserts, ensuring durability and ventilation for both short and long rides.

**Key Features:**
- **Reinforced Knuckle Armor:** Offers impact protection during unexpected situations.
- **Anti-Slip Palm Grip:** Provides superior control of your bike's handlebars, even in wet conditions.
- **Touchscreen Compatibility:** Allows you to operate your smartphone or GPS without removing the gloves.
- **Adjustable Wrist Strap:** Ensures a snug fit for maximum comfort and safety.
- **Water-Resistant Coating:** Protects against light rain, keeping your hands dry and comfortable.

Available in multiple sizes (S, M, L, XL), the **Fennor Riding Gloves** are perfect for all types of riders, offering a stylish design that doesn't compromise on functionality. Elevate your riding gear with gloves that work as hard as you do.
