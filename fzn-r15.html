FZN-R15

The FZN-R15 is a perfect blend of cutting-edge technology, dynamic design, and outstanding performance. Designed for riders who value both style and substance, this motorcycle offers a thrilling riding experience with its sleek and aerodynamic build. Whether for daily commutes or weekend adventures, the YZF-R15 stands out with its advanced features, sporty appeal, and a commitment to delivering both comfort and excitement on every journey.

Features

Powerful 155cc Engine with VVA

A robust 155cc liquid-cooled engine with SOHC, 4-valves, and fuel injection technology. The Variable Valve Actuation (VVA) system ensures an exceptional riding experience.

Dual Channel Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS)

The dual-channel ABS enhances braking control, ensuring optimal safety and performance.

Assist & Slipper Clutch

The assist feature lightens the clutch operation, while the slipper clutch ensures smoother gear shifts and faster acceleration.

M-Shaped Intake Duct with LED Projector Headlight

A signature R-Series front design featuring an "M"-shaped air intake inspired by the YZR-M1, complemented by an LED projector headlight for superior illumination.

Sporty Riding Position with Aerodynamic Design

Offers a sporty and racing-inspired riding position with a fairing design that boasts exceptional aerodynamics, embodying the R-DNA.

Fenor Motorcycle Connect

Equipped with Communication Control Unit (CCU) technology, the first in Indonesian-made motorcycles, allowing seamless connection to the Y-Connect app via Bluetooth.
