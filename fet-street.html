Features

Engine Specifications

The Fiora 125 is powered by a 109.5cc, 4-stroke, SOHC eSP engine with a compression ratio of 10.0:1. It delivers 6.6 kW (9.0 PS) at 7,500 rpm and 9.2 Nm of torque at 6,000 rpm. The engine features automatic centrifugal dry clutch, electric starter, PGM-FI fuel injection, and V-Matic transmission. Additionally, it uses a full transistorized ignition system and NGK MR9C-9N spark plugs for reliable performance.

Anti-Theft Alarm

An anti-theft alarm system designed to provide extra security when parking the motorcycle, helping to deter theft and protect your vehicle.

Chassis and Suspension

The motorcycle features a Backbone frame with the eSAF (enhanced Smart Architecture Frame) for improved stability and strength. It is equipped with a telescopic front suspension and a swingarm rear suspension with a single shock absorber. The tires are tubeless, with a 100/90-12 59J size at the front and 110/90-12 64J at the rear. For braking, the motorcycle uses a single-piston hydraulic disc brake at the front and a drum brake at the rear, paired with a Combi Brake System for balanced braking performance.

LED Lighting System

The modern LED lighting system enhances the bike's appearance, adding a sleek and stylish touch to its overall design.

Full Digital Panel Meter

Ride in style with the advanced digital panel meter, featuring an ECO indicator light for convenient and efficient ride information.

Anti-Theft Alarm

An advanced security system that provides extra protection when parking your motorcycle, ensuring peace of mind with enhanced safety features.
